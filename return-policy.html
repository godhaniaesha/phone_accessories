Returns & Refunds

Simple, honest, and easy. Just the way it should be.

1. Eligibility for Returns

We want you to love your purchase. If you're not completely satisfied, we accept returns within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unused, in their original aesthetic packaging, and in the same condition that you received them.

2. Non-Returnable Items

  • Used or damaged products.
  • Items without original packaging.
  • Customized or clearance items.
  • Gift cards or promotional freebies.

3. Return Process

  • Contact our support with your Order ID.
  • Share clear product images via email.
  • Once approved, we'll arrange a pickup.
  • Pack securely in original box.

4. Refund Policy

After receiving and inspecting your return, we'll notify you of the approval. Approved refunds are processed within 7–10 business days directly to your original payment method.

5. Replacement Policy

Received something broken? We’ll fix it! For any defective items, we offer a free replacement. Just let us know within 48 hours of delivery.

6. Shipping Charges

Original shipping charges are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping will be deducted unless the error was on our part.

7. Cancellation Policy

Changed your mind? You can cancel your order anytime before it leaves our studio. Once dispatched, our standard return policy will apply.
